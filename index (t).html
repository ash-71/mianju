Policy

Returns & Refunds

Our Commitment

We want you to love your MIANJU purchase. If your item arrives damaged, faulty, or significantly different from its description, contact us within 7 days of delivery and we will arrange a refund, replacement, or store credit as appropriate.

Change of Mind

As a small independent label, change-of-mind returns are not accepted at this time. We encourage you to review sizing carefully before placing your order. Our team is happy to assist with fit questions before purchase.

Return Shipping

For eligible returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked service. MIANJU is not liable for items lost in transit on return.

Pre-Order Items

As pieces are produced specifically in response to demand, pre-orders cannot be cancelled once production has commenced.

How to Request

Email hello@mianju.com.au with your order number and photographs of the issue within 7 days of receiving your order. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to your original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation once your refund has been issued.
